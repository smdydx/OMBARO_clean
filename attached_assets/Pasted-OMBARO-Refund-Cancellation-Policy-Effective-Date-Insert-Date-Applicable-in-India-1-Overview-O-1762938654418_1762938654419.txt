OMBARO Refund & Cancellation Policy
Effective Date: [Insert Date]
Applicable in: India
1. Overview
OMBARO provides a digital platform that connects customers with verified beauticians, freelancers, and
partner salons for at-home beauty and wellness services.
As OMBARO acts as a facilitator between customers and service providers, refunds and cancellations are
subject to both Platform Terms and the Provider’s service status at the time of request.
2. Customer Cancellations
a. Before Service Confirmation
• You may cancel your booking anytime before a provider is assigned, at no charge.
• Full refund (if prepaid) will be issued to your original payment method or OMBARO Wallet
within 7–10 business days.
b. After Service Confirmation (Before Start Time)
• If you cancel after a beautician/salon is assigned, but before they start traveling, a nominal
cancellation fee (TBD) may apply to cover scheduling costs.
• Refund will be processed after deduction of this fee.
c. After Service Professional Starts Traveling
• If the professional has started traveling or arrived at your location, a 50% service charge may
apply.
• The remaining amount will be refunded to your OMBARO Wallet within 5–7 business days.
d. After Service Has Started
• Once the service begins, no refund is applicable except in cases of verified dissatisfaction,
hygiene failure, or service quality issue (see Section 5).
3. Provider Cancellations
• If a provider cancels due to unavailability, illness, or other valid reasons, OMBARO will:
o Offer an alternate professional, or
o Provide a 100% refund to your original payment method or OMBARO Wallet within 5–7
business days.
• Repeated cancellations by a provider will be subject to disciplinary action on our platform.
4. Product Orders (Monodose Kits & Beauty Products)
For purchases made on the OMBARO platform:
• Cancellations are allowed before dispatch with a full refund.
• Once shipped, products can be returned only if damaged, defective, or incorrect.
• Refunds or replacements are processed within 7–10 business days after the returned item is
received and inspected.
• Opened, used, or tampered products are not eligible for return for hygiene reasons.
5. Refunds Due to Service Issues
Customers may request refunds in cases such as:
• Poor hygiene or non-use of Monodose kits
• Incomplete or unsatisfactory service
• Overcharging or incorrect billing
Resolution Process:
1. Submit your complaint within 24 hours of service completion via the app, website, or email
(support@ombaro.com).
2. Provide pictures, videos, or order ID for review.
3. OMBARO will review the case and may offer:
o Full or partial refund,
o Free re-service, or
o Service credits in OMBARO Wallet.
Refunds are issued within 7–10 business days after claim verification.
6. Mode of Refund
• Refunds are credited to the original payment source (UPI, card, net banking, wallet).
• If payment was made in cash, refunds are issued to the OMBARO Wallet.
• Wallet credits are valid for 180 days from the date of issue.
7. Non-Refundable Situations
No refund is provided in the following cases:
• Change of mind after service completion.
• Unavailability of customer at the time of appointment.
• Service dissatisfaction not backed by valid proof.
• Repeated cancellations by the same user.
8. For Providers (Beauticians / Salons)
• Cancellations by providers less than 1 hour before service time may result in penalties or
deductions from future payouts.
• Refunds to customers in such cases are borne by the provider as per OMBARO’s partner policy.
9. Processing Time
All approved refunds are processed within 5–10 working days, depending on your bank or payment
provider. OMBARO is not responsible for delays due to third-party payment processors.

All refund and cancellation matters are governed by the laws of India.
Disputes, if any, shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Courts of Mumbai, India.
